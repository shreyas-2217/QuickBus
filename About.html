Our History

QuickBus was founded in 2018 with a simple goal: to make bus travel more accessible, comfortable, and affordable. The journey began with a small fleet of buses operating between a few cities. Over the years, we have expanded our network to cover over 100+ destinations across the country, ensuring that you can travel to your desired location with ease.

In our early days, QuickBus was known for its dedication to customer satisfaction and safety. As we grew, we introduced several innovations, including online booking, luxury buses, and special discounts for students and senior citizens. Our commitment to excellence has made us one of the most trusted bus services in the country.

Our Milestones
